Why we collect these recordings

We are building robots for warehouses and factories.

To train them, we need real recordings of how people perform everyday physical tasks in real working environments.

These recordings help robots learn how real-world work actually happens - directly from human behavior.

Who can apply

You can apply if:

  • You work in a warehouse, factory, manufacturing, or similar environment
  • You have a smartphone
  • You are comfortable wearing or mounting your phone during work

We care about real work footage - not perfect filming.

Frequently asked questions

What kind of work should I record?

We are looking for normal day-to-day physical work in real environments. This includes packing, sorting, stocking, moving items, scanning products, assembly work, and similar tasks. The most important thing is that the work is real and natural.

Do I need special equipment?

No. You only need a smartphone with a working camera. We will explain how to securely mount your phone so you can work normally while recording.

Do I need to edit the video?

No editing is required. Simply upload the full recording after your shift using the secure link we send you.

How long should the recording be?

We pay $15 for every 1 hour of usable footage. Longer recordings may qualify for additional payments.

How do payments work?

Payments are sent through a secure payout system after your footage is reviewed. Most participants receive payment within 1-3 business days.

What kind of phone can I use?

Most modern smartphones work well. As long as your phone can record clear video (1080p or higher recommended), it should be compatible.

What makes a good recording?

Good recordings clearly show your hands, the objects you work with, and your work environment. No professional filming skills are needed - normal work footage is exactly what we are looking for.

Why are these recordings useful?

We use these recordings to train robots for warehouses and factories. By learning from real human work, robots can better understand how tasks are performed in real environments.

Is my information secure?

Yes. We treat all recordings and application data with high confidentiality. Your recordings are used only for this program, videos are not published or shared publicly, access is limited to authorized team members only, personal identifiers such as faces may be automatically blurred during processing, and data is used strictly for training and improving our systems.

Who can participate?

Anyone working in a warehouse, factory, manufacturing, or similar environment can apply. No previous experience with filming or AI is required.
