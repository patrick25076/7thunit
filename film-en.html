Get paid to film your work

Help build the robots that will work alongside you.

7th Unit is collecting real-world workflow footage to train the next generation of humanoid robots. We pay workers to film a single shift of their normal job, from their own perspective. No script. No staging. Just your work, as you do it.

How it works

01

Apply.

Fill out the short form below. Tell us what kind of work you do and where. We're looking for manufacturing, warehousing, logistics, construction, and similar physical roles.

02

Get the head-mounted camera.

If selected, we ship you a small head-mounted camera with simple instructions. It's lightweight, hands-free, and recharges in under an hour.

03

Film a normal shift.

Wear the camera through one regular working day. Don't perform, don't change anything about how you work. The whole point is that the footage reflects real conditions.

04

Upload and get paid.

Send the camera back with the footage. We review it, pay you for your time, and use the data to teach our robots how the work actually gets done.

Apply

Tell us about your work.

Submissions are reviewed weekly. We pay in USD via international wire or local equivalent.
