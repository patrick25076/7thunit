Product

A humanoid worker for facilities built around people.

Traditional automation replaces a single task. A conveyor moves one thing. A robotic arm performs one operation. 7th Unit is built to replace the part of the shift that still depends on a human body moving through an existing facility.

Deployment profile

Designed for high-friction industrial work.

01 physical task layer

Navigation, handling, and repeatable workflows.

The platform combines mobility, manipulation, contextual awareness, and a controlled skill library for multi-step work across a full day.

A Move inventory, tools, and materials through human-scale environments.
B Handle repetitive transfer, sorting, and staging tasks.
C Report completion, exceptions, and facility observations through Unit OS.
02 fleet learning

The robot improves through real workflows.

We are not designing for isolated lab demonstrations. Every pilot is structured around measurable facility work: hours logged, tasks completed, intervention rate, and cost saved.

01 Map the workflow with your operations team.
02 Train and validate against the actual facility conditions.
03 Deploy, measure, and iterate on real shift data.
